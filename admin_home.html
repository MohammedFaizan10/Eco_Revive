About EcoRevive

EcoRevive is dedicated to promoting sustainability by providing a platform for users to easily donate unwanted items, sell scrap materials, and find reliable repair services. Our goal is to reduce waste, extend the lifecycle of products, and reward users for their eco-conscious actions. This admin panel provides tools to manage the data generated through these services, ensuring smooth operation and effective community support.
