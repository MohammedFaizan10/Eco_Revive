Support Trusted NGOs Directly

You can also choose to donate directly to these reputable organizations making a significant impact.

UNICEF India

Works relentlessly for the rights and well-being of every child across India, focusing on critical areas like health, nutrition, education, and protection from violence and exploitation.

They accept: Monetary Donations
Donate to UNICEF

Donatekart

India's unique platform connecting donors directly with NGOs needing specific products for their beneficiaries. Ensures transparency by allowing donations of essential items rather than just money.

They accept: Products & Essentials
Donate via Donatekart

Digital Daan (DEF)

An initiative by the Digital Empowerment Foundation aimed at bridging India's digital divide. They collect and refurbish old smartphones, tablets, and laptops for underprivileged students.

They accept: Digital Devices
Donate Devices

DonateInKind

Facilitates the donation of goods by connecting individuals and corporates with verified NGOs across India, ensuring that items reach those genuinely in need.

They accept: Food, Clothes, Furniture
Donate Goods In Kind

The Salvation Army

An international movement and evangelical part of the universal Christian Church. Provides spiritual and material aid to people in need without discrimination worldwide.

They accept: Monetary Donations, Supplies
